Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup crushed Ritz crackers or similar butter crackers
  • 1/2 cup butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 Teaspoon garlic salt
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°. Lightly spray a 9x13 baking dish with non-stick spray.
  2. Place crushed crackers in a medium sized shallow bowl.
  3. Add garlic salt and pepper to the bowl and mix.
  4. In another bowl, add the beaten eggs.
  5. One at a time, dip chicken breasts into eggs, then drege in the cracker crumbs, coating on all sides.
  6. Place chicken breasts side-by-side in baking dish.
  7. Top chicken evenly with pieces of butter.
  8. Bake on middle rack for 40-45 minutes, or until chicken has reached an internal temperature of 165°.
