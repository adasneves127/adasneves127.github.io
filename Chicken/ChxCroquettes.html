Chicken:

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Warsetershire
  • 1/2 cup mayonaise
  • 2 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt + pepper
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Onion - Sautéed
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Celery - Sautéed

Instructions

  1. Mince 4 cooked chicken breasts
  2. Mix with above ingredients - Dredge in panko + fry in 1/2" oil
