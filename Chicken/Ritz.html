Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Real Mayo Mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • 25 RITZ Crackers, finely crushed (about 1 cup)
  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tsp. dried basil leaves
  • 4 small boneless skinless chicken breasts (1 lb.)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400°F.
  2. Mix mayo and lemon juice in medium bowl. Combine cracker crumbs, cheese and basil in separate bowl.
  3. DIP chicken, 1 breast at a time, in mayo mixture, then roll in crumb mixture until evenly coated. Place on baking sheet
  4. sprayed with cooking spray.
  5. Bake 20 min. or until done (165°F).
