Ingredients

7 Eggs1 package pre-cooked bacon1 cup shredded cheese
2 Tbsp Milk1 can Pillsbury Grands Flaky BiscuitsSalt and pepper

Instructions

Bake at 350Â° in a 12x8 glass casserole dish

  1. Bead eggs & milk until smooth. Add salt & pepper
  2. Seperate biscuit dough & cut each biscuit into 4 quarters.
  3. Gently stir biscuit pieces into egg mixture
  4. Add Bacon & cheese
  5. Pour into casserole dish making sure biscuits are in one layer.
  6. Bake for 20-30 minutes or until golden brown.
